Rooted in one coast, carried to your kitchen

Malabar Roots began with a single pepper vine and a promise: that the families who grow the world's finest spices should be the ones who profit from them.

Four decades on, we still work the same hillsides — buying direct, paying fair, and shipping nothing we wouldn't cook with ourselves. No middlemen, no blending, no shortcuts. Just what the land gives, when it's ready.
